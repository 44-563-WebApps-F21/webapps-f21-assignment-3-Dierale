I'm secretly known as AVERAGE MAN

Average Man is a super hero known for being able to do anything!
Except everything he does comes out painfully average.
Need your homework done? Done in in a jiffy! But it's a C.
There's a robbery? I'll be there, just after the cops are already done catching the guy.
Is your cat stuck in an obstacle course of weird trees? I'll get there but it'll take me a while.
I always finish the race! I just don't finish first.
