My Arch Nemesis - THE ORANGE DANGLER

Little is known about my elusive arch foe other than his calling card. You'll find the Orange Dangler himself hanging off any belltower he can find. He finds his dangling post during sunrise or sunset so that he can blend in with his orange camouflage. Once he's in position he repeatedly rings the bells in these belltowers to irrate the townsfolk to no end. His unique coloration and perfect timing make him impossible to catch, and by the time the authorities arrive, he's already off on another belltower ringing away. When the light finally comes or the dark descends he sets off into the night, lost in the wind until his next ringing spree. Some say you can find him hidden deep in light.


FINDING THE DANGLER

As stated the Dangler himself hides deep in the light of the sun to avoid capture. But that secretly is also his bane. If you follow the right directions you'll be able to find his hidden lair.

  • Face the sunset/sunrise (featured below)
  • Spin in a circle
  • Chant "Bingly Bangly Where's the Dangly" three times
  • Dash into the sunset until the light opens up
  • Enter the Dangler Zone
  • You've found him in his cave of dangling dangers
  • Prepare for the chase
