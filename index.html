My Favorite Place: The Mountains of New Mexico

The mountains of New Mexico are one of my favorite places in the world. If we want to be specific, the Sangre de Cristo Mountains of the Rocky Mountains is the most accurate description. I visited them with my father when I was younger and I treasure the memories I have of that.



Travel Directions to New Mexico

  1. Get on US-71 S from Maryville, MO
    1. Take US-71 S
    2. Take I-229 S
    3. Merge into MO-759 S
    4. Turn into US-59 S
    5. Enter Kansas
    6. Continue onto KS-4 W
    7. Merge into US-24 W
    8. Merge onto US-75
    9. Exit onto I-70 W
    10. Turn onto KS-156 W
  2. Continue on US-71. Take US-59 S, KS-4W and I-70 W to KS-156 W in Garfield. Take exit 225 from I-70 W
  3. Continue on US-56 W. Drive to US-64 W in Colfax County
  4. Arrive at Philmont Scout Ranch, New Mexico

Travel Supplies

  • General camping supplies
    • Tent for shelter
    • Sleeping bags and/or blankets
    • Pillow
    • Flaslight & backpack batteries
    • Camping stove or tools to cook
  • Appropriate clothing
    • Loose clothing for active days
    • Warm clothing/jackets for cold nights
    • Rain jacket
  • Hiking equipment
    • Hiking boots
    • Tough/thick socks
    • Hiking backpack
    • Water Bottle (3L or three 1L)
  • Tioletries & Shower Supplies
  • Eating Utensils (plastic cup/spoon)
  • Sunscreen & Bug Spray
  • Bear bags, line, carabiner
